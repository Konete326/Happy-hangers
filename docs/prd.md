# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview
We are developing a robust **Inventory & POS Management System** tailored specifically for a **clothing company**. The system is designed to streamline stock management, product categorization, and point-of-sale operations. Its flagship feature is an end-to-end barcode generation, scanning, and billing workflow that ensures fast and error-free checkouts.

## 2. Core Objectives
* Provide a comprehensive and professional system for managing clothing inventory.
* Automate product identification through system-generated barcodes.
* Eliminate manual billing errors via barcode scanning.
* Provide real-time insights through professional dashboards.

## 3. Key Features

### 3.1 Product & Stock Management
* **Product Catalog:** Ability to add, edit, and manage products with details such as Name, Price, Category, and SKU (Stock Keeping Unit).
* **Categories:** Create and manage product categories (e.g., Shirts, Pants, Accessories).
* **Inventory Tracking:** Proper, robust stock management that automatically deducts stock upon successful sale and alerts on low inventory.

### 3.2 Barcode Generation & Printing
* **SKU/Barcode Generation:** Every added product will have a unique barcode generated by the system.
* **Sticker Printing (80mm):** The system will be compatible with standard thermal receipt/sticker printers **(80mm page size)**.
* **Process:** The system will print out barcode stickers for the required quantity of products. These stickers will then be physically attached to the clothing items (e.g., shirts).

### 3.3 POS (Point of Sale) & Barcode Scanning
* **Scanner Integration:** The interface will seamlessly integrate with physical barcode scanners.
* **Automated Checkout:** When an item is scanned at the counter, the system will instantly fetch its price, name, and details, adding it to the cart.
* **Bill Generation:** Upon checkout, the system will immediately generate and print a customer receipt/bill using the printer.

### 3.4 Dashboards & Analytics
* **Proper Dashboards:** Visual representations of daily sales, total available stock, top-selling categories, and low-stock items.

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements
* **Reliability:** The scanning and deduction of stock must be highly accurate with no calculation mistakes.
* **Hardware Compatibility:** Must communicate seamlessly with 80mm thermal printers and standard USB/Bluetooth barcode scanners.
* **Scalability:** The architecture must cleanly handle future updates and increasing product catalogs.
